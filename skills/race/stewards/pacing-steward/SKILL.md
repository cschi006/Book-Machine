# Pacing Steward
**Sector:** Race · Stewards Panel · **Status:** Stub — ready to build  
**Jurisdiction:** Scene-level pacing — word count, tension arc, energy curve, promise advancement

---

## Purpose

The Pacing Steward reads against the tension curve and telemetry targets from the Story Engineer's dossier. It catches scenes that missed their energy targets — too long, too short, wrong tension level, or failing to advance any promise.

This steward thinks in numbers and curves, not words. Its report is the most quantitative in the panel.

---

## Severity Calls

| Finding | Default severity |
|---|---|
| Scene more than 30% over target word count with no tension gain | Pit stop |
| Scene ends at lower tension than it started with no structural reason | Pit stop |
| Scene advances no promise and serves no arc movement | Investigation |
| Scene more than 15% over/under word count target | Yellow flag |
| Tension curve dip in wrong place for act position | Yellow flag |

---

## Inputs

- Finalized scene draft
- Outline node for this scene (tension arc target, word count target, promise(s) this scene serves)
- Tension curve from Story Engineer dossier
- Current act position

---

## Process

1. Count scene word count — compare to target (±200 word tolerance)
2. Map tension arc: does the scene open at the right tension level? Does it close higher, lower, or at a cliff?
3. Check promise advancement: which promise(s) did this scene serve? Did it advance them?
4. Check act position: does this scene's energy level fit the manuscript's overall tension curve at this point?
5. Flag any anomalies with severity
6. Pass to Team Principal

---
*Build notes: Needs the tension curve format from the Story Engineer output so it knows what it's measuring against. The word count math is simple; the tension judgment requires the dossier context.*
