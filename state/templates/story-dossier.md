## section_3_protagonist_operating_systems

[Build the full NPE Character Operating System for each protagonist. These are the deep psychological profiles that drive every decision, reaction, and transformation in the story. Without these, AI writes flat, reactive characters who make convenient choices. Use the brain_dump for any details the author provided, and invent what is missing to create a coherent, compelling character. Target 1000-2000 words total across all protagonists.]

### protagonist_1

**character_name:** [Name of the first protagonist]

**role:** [Their role in the story — main POV character, co-lead, etc.]

**age:** [Age or age range]

**physical_description:** [Brief physical description — focus on distinctive features and how they carry themselves]

#### operating_system

**core_wound:** [The deep emotional injury that drives their behavior. This is the thing that happened that broke their ability to trust themselves or the world.]

**mask:** [The protective persona they wear to avoid being hurt again. How do they present to the world vs. who they really are?]

**want:** [What they consciously think they need to be happy or whole]

**need:** [What they actually need to heal — this is usually the opposite of what the mask protects]

**fear:** [Their deepest internal terror — the thing they will do almost anything to avoid facing]

**decision_heuristics:** [How do they make choices? Fast and instinctive? Overthinking? Based on duty? Based on self-preservation? Describe their default decision-making pattern.]

**stress_response:** [Fight, flight, freeze, or fawn? How does this character behave when cornered or overwhelmed?]

**contradiction_pattern:** [Their flaw loop — the self-defeating behavior they repeat. Example: "Pushes people away to avoid abandonment, which causes the abandonment they fear."]
